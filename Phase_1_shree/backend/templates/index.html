Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import leads into the system?

+

You can import leads by using our Bulk Import feature. Navigate to "Bulk Import" in the sidebar, upload your CSV file, and map your columns to the appropriate fields in our system. The system will then process your file and import valid leads.

What file formats are supported for importing leads?

+

Currently, the system supports CSV (Comma Separated Values) files for importing leads. Make sure your CSV file includes at least the required fields: Email and Phone number.

How can I export my leads?

+

To export leads, go to the Leads page, select the leads you want to export using the checkboxes, choose your preferred format (CSV or Excel), and click the "Export Selected" button.

Can I update lead status in bulk?

+

Currently, lead status must be updated individually. You can update a lead's status by selecting the appropriate status from the dropdown menu in the Actions column for that lead.

Who can add or delete leads?

+

Only users with Admin or Developer roles can add new leads individually or delete leads. Standard users can view leads, update their status, and export data.

How do I filter leads to find specific information?

+

You can use the filtering options on the Leads page to narrow down your results. Filter by company, location, role/title, lead score, industry, business type, state, employee size, or revenue range to find specific leads.
